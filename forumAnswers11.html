"What are some good substitutes for vanilla extract?"

Tammy B.

If you have a vanilla bean, you can use it as a substitute for extract in a cookie recipe. You can scoop out the seeds of the vanilla bean and add them to melted butter or any other liquid ingredient. Or, you can whisk the beans into the sugar.
