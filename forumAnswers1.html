"How to make egg whites fluffy without a hand-mixer?"

Results for your question:

Sarah V.

I make my eggs fluffy by using a large whisk. Whisk in a circular motion to bring air into the egg whites.

Kayla L.

I do the same! Whisk in a circular motion vertically, not stirring around the bowl. That brings in the air as you lift the whisk each time.
